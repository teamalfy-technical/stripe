Tuaneka Payments powered by

With the Stripe integration, your customers can pay your invoices using their preferred credit or debit card – or with Apple Pay. You’ll be joining millions of businesses around the world that use Stripe to process hundreds of billions of dollars in transactions each year.

Watch demo here

Take the Hustle out of chasing payments

You can use tuaneka to set up recurring payments on your stripe account. This means you can automatically create repeat bills for your clients every time without your client having to keep track of your invoices and you get paid on time every time, providing there is money on the clients account. Watch this demo video to see how it works

Watch demo

Account for every Stripe Transaction

Whenever you receive payments on Stripe, via Tuaneka invoices you’ll automatically see the transaction data flow into Tuaneka using the Stripe feed. Payments and fees are automatically matched to the correct invoices and accounts so the transaction is reconciled and your business data is more insightful.

Add Stripe to Tuaneka

Connect your Stripe account to Tuaneka with just a few clicks. If you don’t have a Stripe account, you can easily create one from within Tuaneka.
